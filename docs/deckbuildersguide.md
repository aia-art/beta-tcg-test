# DOCUMENT 6: DECK BUILDER'S GUIDE
## How to Customise Your Starter Decks

---

## INTRODUCTION

The two starter decks are complete and ready to play. They are also starting points for customisation.

---

## DECK BUILDING RULES (QUICK DUEL)

- **40 cards total per deck** — this includes the Creator card and 2 guaranteed Model cards
- **Creator card** — 1, kept outside the deck during play, not shuffled
- **Guaranteed Model cards** — exactly 2, set aside before shuffling, not counted in the shuffled deck
- **Shuffled deck** — exactly 37 cards
- **Maximum 3 copies** of any single non-Creator card
- Additional copies of Model cards beyond the 2 guaranteed may be included in the 37-card shuffled deck within the 3-copy limit

---

## UNDERSTANDING YOUR CREATOR FIRST

**Aia** wants Coherent in hand early (guaranteed), time to build CLIP-LOCKed Creations, Glitch-dealing tools for opponent Creations, Glitch removal through Remix, and Reputation toward Copy That!

**Anonymous User** wants cheap fast generation, Portrait Creations for Copycat synergy, Visibility pressure for Going Viral, and Quality-boosting Prompts to keep SD1.5 Creations alive.

## CUSTOMISING THE HORDE DECK

**Strengths:** Early aggression, Visibility pressure, Loyalty damage.
**Weaknesses:** Requires Quality-boosting Prompts for SD1.5; vulnerable to board wipes.

Consider adding: More Quality-boosting Prompts, Visibility boosters, direct damage Events.
Consider cutting: Expensive cards above 3 Credits, slow setup cards.

---

## CUSTOMISING THE CLIP DECK

**Strengths:** Protected Creations, high Quality, strong late-game.
**Weaknesses:** Slow setup; going first means Juggernaut not Coherent on Turn 1.

Consider adding: Glitch-dealing cards for opponents, Queue disruption Events, Glitch removal tools.
Consider cutting: Portrait-focused cards, Prompts incompatible with Fantasy or Landscape.

---

## DECK ARCHETYPES IN FIRST RENDER

**Pure Control (Aia):** Protect. Lock. Overrender. Win with Copy That!

**Pure Aggression (Anon):** Generate. Flood. Push Visibility. Win with Going Viral before opponent stabilises.

**Hybrid Disruption:** Anon's speed with disruption Events to deny opponent's game plan.

---

## A NOTE ON THE ASTRONAUT

The most powerful card in Set 001. Every deck runs one copy. Plan for the detach penalty — have Glitch removal or robust Creations ready when it expires.

---

## A NOTE ON STYLE COMPATIBILITY

Remember that Style compatibility bonuses (+1 Quality for compatible, +1 Glitch for incompatible) are game rules, not printed on the Model cards. When building your deck, consider which Styles your Models favour. A deck built around a Model's compatible Styles will generate stronger Creations without needing as many Quality-boosting Prompts.

## DESIGN IDEA — FOR FUTURE CONSIDERATION

*Noted during v4 development:* An alternative Remix rule was considered where a Creation could enter the Remix Queue with Quality 0 by paying the Remix cost, bypassing immediate destruction. This was set aside as it was judged to benefit aggressive decks too much at this stage. Worth revisiting in future sets or formats.
