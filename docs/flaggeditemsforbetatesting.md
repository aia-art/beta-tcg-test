# DOCUMENT 7: FLAGGED ITEMS FOR BETA TESTING

---

## FLAG 001 — REPUTATION CAP (currently 20)

Does 20 feel right? Too high makes abilities cheap to spam. Too low makes Signature abilities nearly unreachable. Please test and report: scarcity, overflow frequency, whether 15 would feel tighter.

---

## FLAG 002 — QUALITY SCALE (currently 1–5)

Does Quality reach 0 too often? Does the current Glitch lethality feel right or too punishing? The SD1.5 mandatory destruction mechanic is intentional but may need clearer signposting on the card itself.

---

## FLAG 003 — DRAW RATE (currently 1 card per turn)

Does 1 card per turn feel like enough with a 37-card shuffled deck? Would 2 cards exhaust decks too quickly?

---

## FLAG 004 — GOING VIRAL THRESHOLD DAMAGE

Going Viral triggers on Liked (6) and Featured (10) only. Maximum damage: 6 Loyalty with 3 Creations crossing both thresholds. Does this feel powerful enough to justify 4 Loyalty + 12 Reputation? Does it feel balanced against Aia's 11 Loyalty starting pool?

---

## FLAG 005 — MORE THAN YOU COST (currently 4 Reputation)

A 2-Loyalty swing for 4 Reputation. Does it dominate when Anon has board advantage? Would 5 or 6 feel more appropriate?

---

## FLAG 006 — INSTANT EVENT TIMING

Currently your-turn-only (except Mass Report). A priority and response system is planned for future formats. Did the restriction ever feel frustrating?

---

## FLAG 007 — SD1.5 MANDATORY DESTRUCTION

The intentional SD1.5 mechanic (Quality 1 + 1 Glitch = destroyed without a Quality Prompt or compatible Style). Does it feel like clever design or a hidden punishment? Could a new player understand it from the card alone?

---

## FLAG 008 — AIA GOING FIRST

When Aia goes first (5 Credits with bonus), she can afford Juggernaut (Play 2 + Activate 3 = 5) but not Coherent (Play 3 + Activate 4 = 7). Juggernaut Creations cannot be CLIP-LOCKed, making Turn 1 a tempo play only. Does this feel acceptable, or does it create a frustrating gap between going first and going second for Aia?

---

## FLAG 009 — STYLE COMPATIBILITY SYSTEM

The new compatibility system (compatible = +1 Quality, incompatible = +1 Glitch) is a game rule not printed on the Model cards. Does this cause confusion during play? Should a reminder be added to the Model cards, or is the Quick Reference sufficient?

---

## FLAG 010 — MASS REPORT COST (currently 7 Reputation)

Mass Report costs 7 Reputation and can be played during either player's turn. Does 7 feel right? Too cheap makes Modifiers unreliable. Too expensive makes Mass Report unplayable. Does the Reputation cost (instead of Credits) create interesting decisions?

---

## FLAG 011 — QUEUE LIMIT (currently 2)

Does a Queue limit of 2 feel right for Quick Duel? Does it constrain Anon's flooding strategy too much? Does it make the game feel tighter or restrictive?

---

## FLAG 012 — ONE MODEL ACTIVATION PER TURN

Does the 1 activation per turn limit feel natural? Does it slow the game too much in the early turns? Does it make Prompt Theft's "counts as your activation" trade-off meaningful?
