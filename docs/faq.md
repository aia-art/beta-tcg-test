# DOCUMENT 3: FAQ
## Frequently Asked Questions

*The FAQ takes priority over individual card text. Absolute Rules take priority over everything.*

---

### A. CREDITS AND ECONOMY

**Q: Do I have to pay Credits to play cards from my hand?**
Yes. Every card has a printed Credit cost. Pay it when you play the card. If the cost is 0, it is free but still counts as being played. Some cards (such as Mass Report) cost Reputation instead of Credits.

**Q: Can I carry over Credits above the cap?**
No. Credits above the active cap are lost before carryover is calculated.

**Q: How do Starting Bonuses interact with first and second player Credits?**
Starting Bonuses are applied once at the start of the game, before the first turn. First player gains 4 Credits at the start of Turn 1, plus their Starting Bonus if it is a Credit bonus. Second player gains 6 Credits, plus their Starting Bonus if applicable. Aia gains +1 Credit; Anonymous User gains +1 Reputation instead.

**Q: Does PRO Subscription raise the Credit cap permanently?**
No. It lasts 3 turns. When it expires, the cap returns to 10.

**Q: Does PRO Subscription's expiry always cost 5 Reputation?**
No. The 5 Reputation loss only applies if you had more than 10 Credits at the moment of expiry. If you were at 10 or below, no Reputation is lost.

**Q: What if the 5 Reputation loss brings me to 0 or below?**
Lose 1 Loyalty (flat penalty). Reputation resets to 0.

**Q: Does PRO Subscription's Runtime reduction affect the Remix Queue?**
No. The Remix Queue is independent. PRO Subscription Runtime reduction does not apply to it. A Creation in the Remix Queue always returns at the start of your next turn unless delayed by a Queue effect.

**Q: Can I spend Credits I do not have?**
No. You can only spend Credits currently in your pool.

---

### B. REPUTATION AND VISIBILITY

**Q: What are the Starting Bonuses for each Creator?**
Aia: +1 Credit. Anonymous User: +1 Reputation. These are printed on the Creator cards.

**Q: Can Reputation exceed 20?**
No. Excess is lost immediately from any source.

**Q: Does the Featured burst trigger every time a Creation reaches 10 Visibility?**
No. Once only per Creation, the first time it reaches 10. The +5 Reputation is gained immediately.

**Q: What is the minimum Reputation a Creation generates per turn?**
0. Cannot go below 0 per Creation per turn.

---

### C. CREATOR ABILITIES AND LOYALTY

**Q: Can I activate more than one ability per turn?**
No. One per Creator per turn.

**Q: Do standard abilities cost Loyalty?**
No. Only Signature abilities (⚡) cost Loyalty.

**Q: Can I use Creator abilities in Round 1?**
Only if the specific ability's card text explicitly states it may be used on Turn 1 or in Round 1. Otherwise, no.

**Q: Does Creator Stress apply on Turn 1?**
No. Absolute Rule — exempt on Turn 1.

**Q: Can Loyalty exceed its starting value?**
Yes. No Loyalty cap.

**Q: What happens when a Creator reaches 0 Loyalty?**
The game ends immediately. That player loses.

---

### D. MODELS AND THE QUEUE

**Q: Are guaranteed Model cards part of the shuffled deck?**
No. They are set aside before shuffling. They form part of the opening hand but are never shuffled in and are not affected by mulligans.

**Q: Can my opponent use a Model I played?**
Yes, from Round 2 onwards.

**Q: How many times can I activate a Model per turn?**
Once. Each player may activate a maximum of 1 Model per turn in Quick Duel.

**Q: Does Runtime count during my opponent's turns?**
No. Runtime only reduces at the start of your own turns.

**Q: Does a LoRA affect Creations already in the Queue?**
No. Only Creations generated after attachment.

**Q: What if I want additional copies of a Model in my deck beyond the 2 guaranteed?**
You may include additional copies within the standard 3-copy limit. Those copies are shuffled into the 37-card deck normally.

**Q: How many Creations can I have in my Queue?**
A maximum of 2 in the main Queue (not counting the Remix Queue). The Remix Queue holds a maximum of 1 additional Creation.

---

### E. CREATIONS, GLITCH AND DESTRUCTION

**Q: When does a Creator lose 1 Loyalty from Creation destruction?**
Whenever any Creation they control is destroyed by any means. Absolute Rule — no exceptions.

**Q: If I destroy my own Creation to free a slot, how much Loyalty do I lose?**
1 Loyalty. This is the standard destruction penalty. There is no additional public deletion penalty.

**Q: SD1.5 generates Quality 1 Creations and automatically adds 1 Glitch token. Does that mean every SD1.5 Creation is immediately destroyed?**
Yes, unless you pair the activation with a Prompt that grants at least +1 Quality, or use a compatible Style tag (which grants +1 Quality on entry). This is intentional. SD1.5 is free but requires investment to use effectively.

**Q: Does a Creation in the Remix Queue count for Creator Stress?**
Yes. It counts as being in the Queue for all purposes.

**Q: Can I Remix a Creation with immunity from a Negative Prompt?**
If not CLIP-LOCKed, yes. While in the Remix Queue, its immunities are ignored.

---

### F. PROMPTS

**Q: Can I play two Prompts of the same subtype in one activation?**
No. Maximum 2 Prompts per activation, different subtypes only.

**Q: What subtype is Anonymous User's Favourite Prompt?**
Style. Combinable with Artist, Negative or Atmosphere — not another Style.

**Q: What subtype is Aia's Favourite Prompt?**
Atmosphere. Combinable with Style, Artist or Negative — not another Atmosphere.

**Q: Can I play 2 card Prompts and also use my Favourite Prompt?**
No. Maximum 2 Prompts total including the Favourite Prompt.

---

### G. CLIP-LOCK

**Q: Do Coherent Creations enter with CLIP-LOCK automatically?**
No. CLIP-LOCK must be applied manually each turn during the Main Phase.

**Q: Can any Creator apply CLIP-LOCK?**
Only Creators whose card or a card effect grants CLIP-LOCK access. In First Render, this is Aia via her passive.

**Q: Does CLIP-LOCK protect against Artifact effects?**
No. Field-wide Artifacts and area Events still apply.

**Q: Does the turn CLIP-LOCK is applied count toward Positive Feedback?**
No. Counting begins the turn after application.

**Q: Does CLIP-LOCK block The Astronaut's detach Glitch token penalty?**
No. CLIP-LOCK only blocks Glitch tokens from opponent sources. The Astronaut is your own card — its detach penalty applies through CLIP-LOCK.

**Q: Can I apply CLIP-LOCK to a Creation that already has Glitch tokens?**
Yes. CLIP-LOCK prevents future opponent Glitch tokens but does not remove existing ones.

---

### H. MODIFIERS AND LORAS

**Q: Can a Model have more than 1 LoRA in Quick Duel?**
No. Maximum 1 LoRA per Model in Quick Duel.

**Q: What happens when PRO Subscription expires with more than 10 Credits?**
Credits above 10 are lost. You lose 5 Reputation. If Reputation reaches 0 or below, lose 1 Loyalty and reset Reputation to 0.

**Q: What happens when PRO Subscription expires with 10 or fewer Credits?**
Cap returns to 10 with no Credits lost. No Reputation loss.

**Q: Can I attach a LoRA to a Model the opponent played?**
Yes. LoRAs attach to any Model in the Shared Zone.

**Q: Can The Astronaut be countered by Mass Report?**
No. The Astronaut cannot be removed, negated or targeted by any card effect. Mass Report explicitly excludes The Astronaut.

**Q: Can Modifiers be played in Round 1?**
No. All Modifiers are restricted in Round 1.

---

### I. ARTIFACTS AND EVENTS

**Q: Can Artifacts be played in Round 1?**
No.

**Q: Can Instant Events be played during the opponent's turn?**
No, with one exception: Mass Report may be played during either player's turn.

**Q: Can I play an Instant Event during my Refresh Phase?**
Yes. Any point during your turn.

**Q: Does Server Overload affect both players?**
Yes.

**Q: Can Server Overload be removed?**
No. It is intentionally not removable. Its high play cost (8 Credits) reflects this.

**Q: Does Generation Cancelled refund Credits?**
No.

**Q: Does Algorithm Swap affect Creations generated during its duration?**
Yes. Creations generated while Algorithm Swap is active are assigned the swapped Style tag permanently. Existing Creations on the field or in Queues revert to their original tags when the effect ends.

### J. SPECIFIC CARDS — ANONYMOUS USER

**Q: FIRST POST — Can it be used on Turn 1?**
Yes. This is noted on the card. Anonymous User's Starting Bonus of +1 Reputation provides exactly enough to activate it.

**Q: FIRST POST — Does a Creation in the Remix Queue prevent its use?**
Yes. The Remix Queue counts as the Queue for all purposes. If any Creation is in your Remix Queue, First Post cannot be used.

**Q: GOING VIRAL — Which thresholds trigger Loyalty damage?**
The Liked threshold (reaching 6 Visibility for the first time during this activation) and the Featured threshold (reaching 10 Visibility for the first time during this activation). Noticed (reaching 3) does not trigger Loyalty damage.

**Q: GOING VIRAL — Can a Creation trigger both thresholds in one activation?**
Yes. If a Creation jumps from below 6 to 10 or above, it crosses both Liked and Featured — dealing 2 Loyalty damage from that one Creation.

**Q: GOING VIRAL — What is the maximum Loyalty damage possible?**
With 3 Creations each crossing both Liked and Featured: 6 Loyalty damage total.

**Q: GOING VIRAL — Does the Featured burst also trigger if a Creation reaches 10 for the first time via Going Viral?**
Yes. The +5 Reputation Featured burst and the 1 Loyalty damage both resolve simultaneously. If the opponent Creator reaches 0 Loyalty, the game ends immediately regardless of any Reputation gained.

**Q: COPYCAT — What if the opponent has multiple Creations with the same Style tag?**
The Anonymous User player chooses which one loses 2 Visibility Counters.

**Q: COPYCAT — Can it reduce an opponent Creation's Visibility below 0?**
No. Visibility cannot go below 0. If the target Creation has fewer than 2 Visibility Counters, steal only what is available.

**Q: MORE THAN YOU — Does the Loyalty transfer happen if the opponent Creator is eliminated?**
No. The game ends immediately at 0 Loyalty.

### K. SPECIFIC CARDS — AIA

**Q: POSITIVE FEEDBACK — Can I use it on a Creation CLIP-LOCKed this turn?**
Yes, but you gain 0 Loyalty since it has been locked for 0 full turns.

**Q: POSITIVE FEEDBACK — How do I track turns locked?**
Place a counter on the Creation Slot each turn during Refresh while CLIP-LOCK is active. The turn CLIP-LOCK is applied does not add a counter.

**Q: OVERRENDER — Does the extra damage apply if Glitch tokens were applied this turn?**
Yes. As long as the Creation has 1 or more Glitch tokens when Overrender resolves, the additional damage applies.

**Q: OVERRENDER — If Overrender destroys a Creation, does the opponent lose 1 Loyalty?**
Yes. Absolute Rule.

**Q: COPY THAT! — Can I use it with fewer than 3 CLIP-LOCKed Creations?**
Yes. Up to 3 means 1, 2 or 3.

**Q: COPY THAT! — Does using only 1 Creation still trigger the Reputation loss?**
No. The 2 Reputation loss only applies if 2 or more Creations target the same Creator.

**Q: IRIDESCENT SHIFT — Does the targeting immunity protect against Going Viral?**
No. Going Viral is not a targeted effect. Iridescent Shift only blocks abilities that specifically single-target the Creation.

### L. SPECIFIC CARDS — OTHER

**Q: THE ASTRONAUT — Can it attach to opponent Creators?**
No. Friendly Creators only.

**Q: THE ASTRONAUT — Does CLIP-LOCK block its detach Glitch token penalty?**
No. See Section G.

**Q: THE ASTRONAUT — Can Mass Report cancel it?**
No. The Astronaut cannot be removed, negated or targeted by any card effect. Mass Report explicitly excludes it.

**Q: DOUBLE DRAGON HEAD — Does the attached Creation's Creator still lose 1 Loyalty if destroyed?**
Yes. Absolute Rule.

**Q: NOISE — Does it affect Creations already on the field?**
No. Only Creations generated after attachment.

**Q: HERE GOES THE PARAGRAPH — Which Models is it compatible with?**
All Models except Coherent variants.

**Q: MASS REPORT — Can it be played during the opponent's turn?**
Yes. Mass Report is the only Instant in First Render that may be played during either player's turn. It must be played in direct response to a Modifier being played, before that Modifier's effects resolve.

**Q: MASS REPORT — It costs Reputation instead of Credits?**
Yes. Mass Report costs 7 Reputation to play. It has no Credit cost. This is intentional — it can be played when you have no Credits available, as long as you have the Reputation.

**Q: MASS REPORT — Can it cancel The Astronaut?**
No. The Astronaut cannot be removed, negated or targeted by any card effect. Mass Report explicitly excludes it.

**Q: MASS REPORT — Can it cancel PRO Subscription?**
No. Mass Report explicitly excludes PRO Subscription.

**Q: MASS REPORT — Can it cancel a Modifier that has already resolved?**
No. It must be played in response to a Modifier being played, before its effects take place.

**Q: PROMPT THEFT — When can I play it?**
During your own Main Phase. It references the opponent's most recent turn. If they activated a Model on that turn, you may copy one of the Prompts they used.

**Q: PROMPT THEFT — Does this count as my activation for the turn?**
Yes. You may not activate another Model this turn.

**Q: PROMPT THEFT — What if the copied Prompt assigns a Style tag that is incompatible with my Model?**
The Creation gains 1 Glitch token on entry from the incompatibility. Any Style may be used with any Model — incompatibility is a penalty, not a prohibition.

### M. STYLE COMPATIBILITY

**Q: Can I assign an incompatible Style tag to a Creation?**
Yes. The Creation gains 1 Glitch token on entry. This is a penalty, not a prohibition.

**Q: Can I assign a compatible Style tag to a Creation?**
Yes. The Creation gains +1 Quality on entry.

**Q: What if the Style tag is neither compatible nor incompatible with the Model?**
No bonus or penalty.

**Q: Do compatibility bonuses and incompatibility penalties stack with Prompt effects?**
Yes. All entry effects are applied simultaneously when the Creation enters the Queue.

**Q: Can a Creation have more than one Style tag?**
In First Render, no card creates a Creation with multiple Style tags. If a future card or effect would assign a second Style tag to a Creation that already has one, the new tag replaces the existing one unless the effect explicitly states otherwise.

---

### N. WINNING AND LOSING

**Q: What if both Creators reach 0 Loyalty simultaneously?**
The player who caused the effect wins. If caused by the same single effect with no active player distinction, the game is a draw.

**Q: If my deck runs out, do I lose?**
Only if you also have no cards in hand simultaneously.

**Q: When is the no-cards check performed?**
Continuously. Loss is immediate whenever the condition is met.

**Q: Can I win with no Creations on the field?**
Yes, as long as your Creator is alive. You take Creator Stress each turn from Turn 2 but can still win through other means.

---

### O. QUEUE TIMEOUT

**Q: QUEUE TIMEOUT — Does it affect Creations already in the Queue?**
Yes. All queued Creations have their Runtime increased by 1 when Queue Timeout enters play, and all Models have +1 Runtime for new Creations while Queue Timeout is active.

---

### P. ALGORITHM SWAP

**Q: ALGORITHM SWAP — Does it change the actual Style tag on the Creation Slot?**
For existing Creations, no — they are treated as having the swapped tag while the effect is active, then revert. For Creations generated during the effect, yes — they are assigned the swapped tag permanently.

**Q: ALGORITHM SWAP — Does the swapped tag affect Style compatibility with Models?**
Yes. If you generate a Creation during the swap, the swapped tag is checked against the Model's compatibility list.

**Q: ALGORITHM SWAP — Can I swap the same two tags that are already swapped by a previous Algorithm Swap?**
Only one Algorithm Swap can be active at a time. If a second is played while the first is still active, the first expires immediately and the second takes effect.
