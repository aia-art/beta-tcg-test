## MEET YOUR CREATOR

**LOYALTY: 11** — Lower than Anonymous User's 16. Aia is not built to absorb punishment. She is built to prevent it.

**FACTION: CLIP Resistance** — Precise, patient, anti-disruption. Fewer Creations, fiercely protected.

**FAVOURITE PROMPT:** *"vibrant expressionism by Aia, soft iridescent water reflections"* — +1 Quality and 1 bonus Visibility Counter. Counts as an Atmosphere Prompt.

**STARTING BONUS: +1 Credit** — Aia begins the game with 1 bonus Credit on top of her normal starting Credits. Going first: 4 + 1 = 5 Credits. Going second: 6 + 1 = 7 Credits. This helps with her more expensive Models.

Set your Creator card centred in front of you. Place 11 Loyalty tokens next to it.

## WHAT MAKES AIA DIFFERENT

Aia plays slow and deep. Her goal is to build a small number of powerful, locked Creations and use them as weapons.

Three things define her playstyle:

1. **Coherent (Low Settings)** — Her primary Model. Expensive but generates Quality 3 Creations with 1 bonus Visibility on entry. Going second (7 Credits), Aia can afford Coherent on Turn 1 (Play 3 + Activate 4 = 7). Going first (5 Credits), she uses Juggernaut v9 instead (Play 2 + Activate 3 = 5) as a tempo play to avoid Creator Stress.

2. **CLIP-LOCK** — A protective state applied manually to Coherent Creations. Blocks opponent Glitch tokens and single-target abilities.

3. **Her abilities** — Building toward Copy That!, her Signature ability.

## CLIP-LOCK — THE CORE MECHANIC

**Aia's passive — CLIP-LOCK MASTERY:**
Once per turn, during your Main Phase, you may apply CLIP-LOCK to one of your active Coherent Creations. This is a deliberate choice — Creations do not enter with CLIP-LOCK automatically.

**While CLIP-LOCKed:**
- Cannot receive Glitch tokens from opponent sources
- Cannot be targeted by opponent single-target abilities
- Can still be affected by field-wide Artifacts and area Events
- Cannot be Remixed
- Your own Glitch token effects still apply

**Tracking:** Place a counter on the Creation Slot each turn during Refresh while CLIP-LOCK is active. The turn you apply CLIP-LOCK does not count. Counting begins the following turn.

## AIA'S ABILITIES

In Round 1, Creator abilities cannot be used unless the specific ability's card text explicitly states it may be used on Turn 1.

**① OVERRENDER — Cost: 3 Reputation**
Target one opponent Creation. It loses 1 Quality. If it already has 1 or more Glitch tokens, it loses 1 additional Quality instead. Quality 0 = destroyed, Creator loses 1 Loyalty.

**② POSITIVE FEEDBACK — Cost: 5 Reputation**
Choose one CLIP-LOCKed Creation and remove its CLIP-LOCK. Gain Loyalty equal to the number of turns it has been CLIP-LOCKed, maximum 3.

**③ IRIDESCENT SHIFT — Cost: 6 Reputation**
Target one of your active Creations. It gains 2 Visibility Counters and cannot be targeted by opponent abilities until the start of your next turn.

**⚡ COPY THAT! — Cost: 4 Loyalty · 14 Reputation**
Choose up to 3 of your active CLIP-LOCKed Creations. Remove CLIP-LOCK from each. Each deals 1 Loyalty damage to target opponent Creator. If 2 or more target the same Creator, that Creator also loses 2 Reputation.

*Your win condition. With 3 CLIP-LOCKed Creations: 3 Loyalty damage plus 2 Reputation loss. Plan toward this.*

## HOW TO WIN WITH AIA

**Stage 1 — Establish your Model**
Going second: play Coherent immediately (7 Credits = Play 3 + Activate 4, exactly). Going first: play Juggernaut (5 Credits = Play 2 + Activate 3, exactly) to avoid Creator Stress, then establish Coherent next turn.

**Stage 2 — Generate and Lock**
Get Coherent Creations on the field. Apply CLIP-LOCK each turn. Build toward 2 or 3 locked Creations.

**Stage 3 — Build Reputation and Recover Loyalty**
Steady Reputation from Quality 3 Creations. Use Positive Feedback to recover Loyalty. Save toward Copy That!

**Stage 4 — Fire Copy That!**
When ready, activate Copy That! with as many CLIP-LOCKed Creations as possible. Rebuild and fire again if needed.

## DEALING WITH THE HORDE DECK

- **Overrender** — Combine with Glitch tokens from your Prompts to destroy opponent Creations efficiently.
- **Generation Cancelled** — Remove queued Creations before they arrive.
- **Queue Crash** — Delay incoming Creations.
- Watch out for **Going Viral** — if your opponent has Creations near 6 or 10 Visibility, disrupt before they can fire.

---

## A NOTE ON LOYALTY

Aia starts with only 11 Loyalty. Every decision matters.

- Do not activate Copy That! unless you are confident it will be decisive.
- Use Positive Feedback regularly to recover Loyalty.
- The Astronaut gives +3 Loyalty on attachment.
- Avoid letting your Creations be destroyed — every one costs 1 Loyalty.
