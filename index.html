Quick Duel Edition · Beta Release

Hello, and thank you.

You are holding one of the very first copies of NightCafe: The Card Game to exist anywhere in the world. You are not just a player — you are part of building something from the ground up.

This is Set 001 — First Render. The first set of NightCafe: The Card Game. Two starter decks, forty cards each, one complete experience.

What I need from you

  • Play it. Tell me what feels wrong. Tell me what feels right. Be honest.
  • Focus on the Reputation economy and the 20-cap
  • Test the SD1.5 destruction mechanic — clever or confusing?
  • Try CLIP-LOCK — rewarding or tedious?
  • Push for Going Viral — satisfying climax or hard to set up?
  • Check the draw rate — enough cards each turn?
  • See Document 7 (Flagged Items) for the full list

A few things to know

  • Rules v0.14. Things will change.
  • FAQ and Absolute Rules take priority over card text.
  • Some mechanics (Evolution, Legacy Models, Gallery War) are not available in this set.
